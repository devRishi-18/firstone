जनकश्चोपनेता च यश्च विद्यां प्रयच्छति।
अन्नदाता भयत्राता पश्चैते पितरः स्मृताः॥

Hindi Translation :
चाहे धनी हो या निर्धन, दुःखी हो या सुखी, निर्दोष हो या सदोष – मित्र ही मनुष्य का सबसे बड़ा सहारा होता है।
English Translation :
Whether rich or poor, grieving or happy, innocent or bastard – friend is the biggest support of man.

Creators of our Universe: TRIMURTI

Brahma (ब्रह्मा) is a Hindu god, referred to as "the Creator" within the Trimurti.
He is associated with creation, knowledge, and the Vedas.

Vishnu is the preserver and protector of the universe.
His role is to return to the earth in troubled times and restore the balance of good and evil.

Shiva is known as "The Destroyer" within the Trimurti.
Shiva is the Supreme Lord who creates, protects and transforms the universe.
